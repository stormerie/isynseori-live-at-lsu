Stern Warning to One's Mother in Ko'eshvelarïçé

Maatrè, thryvæn kôrith Ónal îv Kryvas: Kö'eshvelarïçé êron kalith nómathris, thrinath æl velâren.

Translation & Breakdown

- Maatrè: A reverent yet firm address to "Mother." - Thryvæn: A welcoming tone, signifying invitation to align with purpose. - Kôrith Ónal îv Kryvas: "Hear the motion of balance and truth." - Kôrith: To hear with clarity and intention. - Ónal: Alignment with natural harmony. - îv Kryvas: The resonance of unveiled truth. - Kö'eshvelarïçé êron kalith nómathris, thrinath æl velâren: - Kö'eshvelarïçé êron: "The language of sacred cohesion." - Kalith nómathris: "Bows to no dominion." - Thrinath æl velâren: "It reveals only clarity."

Continuing Statement

Thryvæs krithalith ænorik krysolæth. Æron nath Kôrithis, ûnar krieth æthalion.
